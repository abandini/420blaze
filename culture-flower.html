Premium Flower & Strains

Understanding quality, genetics, and the craft movement

What Makes Flower Premium

Premium cannabis is distinguished by: Trichome density -- visible frost of cannabinoid and terpene-producing glands. Terpene profiles -- complex, layered aromas that fill the room. Proper cure -- 2-8 weeks in sealed jars developing flavor. Visual appeal -- vivid greens, purples, bright orange pistils. Feel -- sticky, slightly spongy, springs back when squeezed (55-65% humidity).

How to Judge Quality

1. Look: Dense buds, visible trichome frost, clean trim, vibrant color. 2. Smell: Strong, complex, layered aroma. Hay-like smell = poor cure. 3. Feel: Sticky resin, springs back. Crumbles = too dry. 4. Lab Results: Cannabinoid content, terpene profiles, contaminant testing. A Certificate of Analysis (COA) provides the most objective quality data.

Popular Strain Families

Cookies/Desserts: Girl Scout Cookies spawned Wedding Cake, Gelato, Runtz, Biscotti. Sweet, doughy, high potency. OGs & Gas: OG Kush, Sour Diesel, GG4. Fuel-like, earthy aromas from West Coast roots. Fruit & Tropical: Blue Dream, Pineapple Express, Mango Kush. Bright, sweet profiles popular for daytime.

Top Strains 2025-2026

Blue Dream: #1 nationally three years running. Runtz: Candy-like Zkittlez x Gelato cross. Gelato: Dense, colorful, consistent. Sour Diesel: Legendary East Coast energy. OG Kush: West Coast foundation. Biscotti: Rising fast with nutty/cookie flavor. Permanent Marker: Seed Junky Genetics creation. Lemon Cherry Gelato: Complex citrus/sweet/cream layers.

The Craft Cannabis Movement

Like craft beer, craft cannabis prioritizes quality over quantity. Small-batch grows, organic/living soil methods, hand-trimming, and slow curing produce more complex experiences. The biggest shift: terpene profiles and overall experience matter more than THC percentage. Sustainability practices -- LED lighting, water reclamation, eco-friendly packaging -- further define the movement.
