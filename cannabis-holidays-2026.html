Cannabis Holidays 2026

Every weed-related holiday, observance, and culturally significant date — with the history, meaning, and how stoners actually celebrate.

Quick Answer

How many cannabis holidays are there? There are nine major cannabis observances recognized internationally: 4/20 (April 20), 7/10 (July 10, Dab Day), Bob Marley Day (Feb 6), Willie Nelson's birthday (April 29), Jack Herer Day (June 18), National CBD Day (August 8), Snoop Dogg's birthday (October 20), Green Wednesday (Wednesday before Thanksgiving), and Winter Solstice. Below is the complete 2026 calendar with dates, origins, and ways to celebrate each.

The Complete 2026 Cannabis Calendar

Cannabis culture has its own calendar that runs parallel to the secular one. Some dates (like 4/20) are universally recognized; others (like Jack Herer Day) live closer to the activist core. All of them are worth knowing if you take cannabis seriously as a culture, not just a substance.

February

Feb 6

Bob Marley Day

Birthday of Robert Nesta Marley (1945–1981), reggae icon and one of the most prominent cannabis advocates of the 20th century. Marley framed cannabis as a sacrament of Rastafari faith, not a recreational drug — a perspective that shaped how a generation thought about marijuana’s role in spirituality.

How to celebrate: Spin Legend or Exodus, light something herbal, and read about the Rastafari movement. Pair with a sativa-leaning strain for the dancing.

April

Apr 20

4/20 Day (2027)

The most famous cannabis holiday on Earth. Originated in 1971 when a group of San Rafael, California high schoolers ("the Waldos") would meet at 4:20 PM to search for an abandoned cannabis crop. The Grateful Dead picked up the term, and by the 1990s "420" had become global shorthand for cannabis itself.

How to celebrate: Dispensaries run their second-biggest sale of the year (after Green Wednesday). Cleveland holds dispensary deal days. 420Blazin Festival 2027 launches in Cleveland April 20, 2027.

Apr 29

Willie Nelson’s Birthday

Country music legend Willie Nelson turns 93 in 2026. Nelson has been one of cannabis culture’s most consistent ambassadors for over six decades, founded Willie's Reserve cannabis brand, and has lobbied publicly for federal legalization. His birthday is informally celebrated as "Willie's Day" by Texan and country-leaning stoners.

How to celebrate: Put on Red Headed Stranger, smoke something old-school (try a classic Hindu Kush or Northern Lights), and watch The Highwaymen.

June

Jun 18

Jack Herer Day

Birthday of Jack Herer (1939–2010), author of The Emperor Wears No Clothes — the cannabis-policy book that arguably did more to put cannabis legalization on the political map than any other text. The "Jack Herer" sativa strain was named in his honor and remains one of the most well-known strains in legal markets.

How to celebrate: Watch the video above. Find Jack Herer at your local dispensary — it’s a classic terpinolene-dominant sativa. Read the first chapter of The Emperor Wears No Clothes. Donate to a cannabis-policy nonprofit.

July

Jul 10

7/10 Dab Day

The concentrate community’s answer to 4/20. The number 710 spells "OIL" upside down (7-1-0), and the date became a celebration of cannabis concentrates — shatter, wax, rosin, live resin, hash, and rich terpene-preserving extracts. It’s the second-biggest cannabis sales day for concentrate-focused dispensaries.

How to celebrate: Try a new concentrate format (live rosin if you haven’t). Full 7/10 Dab Day 2026 guide here — Cleveland dispensary picks, gear, and beginner-friendly explainer.

August

Aug 8

National CBD Day

Annual celebration of cannabidiol — the non-intoxicating cannabinoid that opened cannabis to wellness markets in the late 2010s. Originated by cbdMD in 2018. CBD Day is increasingly a mainstream wellness observance, with retailers running sales and educators pushing public awareness of CBD’s difference from THC.

How to celebrate: Try a CBD product if you haven’t (full-spectrum is more effective than isolate). Read about the entourage effect. Recommend it to a 50+ family member who’s curious but not ready for THC.

October

Oct 20

Snoop Dogg’s Birthday

OCT 20 — SNOOP DOGG, 55

Calvin Cordozar Broadus Jr. turns 55 on October 20, 2026. Snoop has been the most visible cannabis cultural ambassador since the 1990s — Doggystyle, the Death Row years, the lifestyle brand, and now a multi-line cannabis business including Leafs by Snoop. His birthday is informally celebrated by hip-hop-leaning stoners.

How to celebrate: Spin Doggystyle top to bottom. Try a Leafs by Snoop flower if your dispensary carries it. Watch Half Baked — Snoop has the iconic "Scavenger Smoker" cameo.

November

Nov 25

Green Wednesday

The cannabis industry’s version of Black Friday Eve. Falls on the Wednesday before Thanksgiving and is the highest-volume sales day for legal dispensaries. Discounts typically run 30–50% off, with online vaporizer retailers running their best annual sales across the same weekend.

How to celebrate: Stock up. Full Green Wednesday 2026 deals tracker here — POTV vaporizer deals, Cleveland dispensary promotions, national chains.

December

Dec 21

Winter Solstice

The longest night and shortest day of the year. Increasingly observed in cannabis culture as a contemplative holiday — a time to slow down with an indica, sit with the dark, and reflect. Particularly resonant for the wellness-focused side of cannabis culture.

How to celebrate: Pick a heavy indica (Northern Lights, Granddaddy Purple, Bubba Kush). Brew tea. Read something old. Don’t leave the house unless you have to.

Why Track Cannabis Holidays?

Cannabis is not just a substance — it’s a culture with its own calendar, its own ancestors, and its own moments worth marking. Tracking these dates does three useful things:

  • Saves money. Dispensaries run real sales on cannabis holidays. Green Wednesday, 4/20, and 7/10 are typically the three deepest-discount days of the year.
  • Builds cultural literacy. Knowing why 7/10 means OIL or how 4/20 originated turns you from a consumer into a participant. It’s the difference between drinking wine and being a wine person.
  • Marks time meaningfully. If you’re going to have a cannabis practice, having seasonal anchor points gives the practice rhythm. Wake & Bake on Saturdays. Indica on Solstice. New strain on 7/10. It adds up.

FAQ

When is 4/20 in 2027?

4/20 in 2027 falls on Tuesday, April 20, 2027. The 420Blazin Festival in Cleveland is being built around this date.

What is 7/10 Dab Day?

7/10 (July 10) is the cannabis concentrate community’s annual celebration. The number 710 spells "OIL" when read upside down. It’s the second-biggest cannabis sales day after 4/20 for dispensaries with strong concentrate selection.

Is Green Wednesday bigger than 4/20?

For sales volume, yes. Green Wednesday (Wednesday before Thanksgiving) is the highest-volume sales day for legal cannabis dispensaries because of pre-holiday stock-up combined with deep discounts. 4/20 is the highest-volume cultural day — festivals, gatherings, music — but Green Wednesday is bigger commercially.

Are there any state-specific cannabis holidays?

Yes. Each state with legal cannabis tracks its own legalization anniversary, which is often locally observed. Ohio’s recreational cannabis launch (August 6, 2024) is increasingly noted by Cleveland-area dispensaries as an anniversary date.
