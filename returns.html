Returns & Refund Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Our Commitment

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase from 420Blazin.com. If something is not right, we are here to help.

Print-on-Demand Merchandise

Because our apparel and merchandise items (tees, hoodies, beanies, stickers, etc.) are custom printed to order, we cannot accept returns for change of mind, wrong size selection, or buyer's remorse. However, we will gladly replace or refund items that are:

  • Defective: Manufacturing defects, printing errors, or significant quality issues
  • Damaged in transit: Items that arrive torn, stained, or otherwise damaged during shipping
  • Wrong item: You received a different item than what you ordered

To request a replacement or refund for defective, damaged, or incorrect items, contact us within 30 days of delivery with your order number and photos of the issue.

Accessories and Drop-Shipped Items

For accessories (rolling papers, grinders, trays, etc.) fulfilled by our dropship partners:

  • Unopened items may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a refund (minus shipping costs)
  • Defective items will be replaced or refunded at no cost to you
  • Opened consumable items (papers, cones, tips) cannot be returned for hygiene and safety reasons

How to Request a Return or Refund

  1. Contact us with your order number, the item(s) in question, and a description of the issue
  2. Include photos if applicable (defects, damage, wrong item)
  3. We will respond within 2 business days with instructions
  4. Do not ship items back without prior authorization

Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5 to 10 business days. You will receive an email confirmation when your refund has been issued.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Digital products and downloads
  • Gift cards
  • Items marked as "final sale" or "non-returnable"
  • Opened consumable products

Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges at this time. If you need a different size or color, please return the original item (if eligible) and place a new order.

Contact

Questions about returns or refunds? Contact us.
