About 420Blazin

The story behind the brand

Who We Are

420Blazin.com is a cannabis culture media brand founded by Blazin Bill in Cleveland, Ohio. What started as a simple countdown to 4/20 has grown into a comprehensive platform connecting cannabis enthusiasts with events, education, products, and community across the United States.

We believe cannabis culture is about more than consumption. It is about community, education, advocacy, and celebration. From the history of 420 to the science of terpenes, from Cleveland dispensary listings to national festival guides, 420Blazin exists to be the resource we wished we had when we first explored this world.

What We Do

Events: We maintain the most comprehensive listing of 420 events and cannabis festivals across the country, with a special focus on our hometown of Cleveland, Ohio.

Education: Our Cannabis Culture section covers everything from plant science and terpene profiles to rolling techniques and concentrate guides. We believe informed consumers are better consumers.

Products: Our merch store offers cannabis culture apparel designed by and for enthusiasts. We also curate the best rolling accessories, papers, and smoking essentials through our accessories shop.

Community: Through 365 Days of Weed, our daily cannabis companion app, we deliver fresh content every single day of the year.

Publishing: Blazin Bill is the author of WEED: A Senior's Guide to Cannabis, the definitive cannabis reference for adults 60 and older.

Our Values

Education over hype. We provide factual, research-backed information. No medical claims, no exaggeration, no BS.

Compliance first. We take legal requirements seriously. Our products are age-restricted and we comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws.

Community driven. 420Blazin was built by cannabis enthusiasts for cannabis enthusiasts. If you have an event to list, a correction to suggest, or just want to connect, reach out.

Based in Cleveland

420Blazin.com is proudly based in Cleveland, Ohio. The Forest City has a vibrant and growing cannabis community, and we are honored to be part of it. Our Cleveland 420 page is the most detailed guide to cannabis events and dispensaries in Northeast Ohio.
