What is Green Wednesday?

Green Wednesday is the cannabis industry’s version of Black Friday Eve. It falls on the Wednesday before Thanksgiving every year and is the highest-volume sales day for legal cannabis dispensaries in the United States. The combination of pre-holiday stock-up, deep discounts (typically 30–50% off), and the cultural overlap with Black Friday weekend makes it the single biggest day of the year for cannabis retail.

Online vaporizer retailers piggyback on the same weekend, with Planet of the Vapes, Storz & Bickel, and other premium brands running their best sales of the year between Green Wednesday and Cyber Monday. If you’ve been waiting to buy a Storz & Bickel Venty or a Mighty+, this is the week to do it.

How to Shop Green Wednesday Like a Pro

  1. Sign up for loyalty programs in advance — many dispensaries offer extra discounts to members. Start now, not the morning of.
  2. Pre-order the night before — POTV opens pre-orders at 10pm Eastern the night before. First-of-day deals sell out by mid-morning.
  3. Make a list, stick to it — deep discounts trigger impulse buying. Budget before browsing.
  4. Cross-shop categories — flower at dispensaries, vaporizers at POTV, accessories at glass shops. Don’t buy a Mighty+ at retail when POTV has it $50 off.
  5. Read the fine print — some deals require membership, minimum spend, or exclude specific brands. Terms matter.
  6. Don’t wait for Cyber Monday — for popular vaporizers, Green Wednesday IS the deal. Cyber Monday cleans up remainders.
  7. Pair flower buys with the right gear — if you’re stocking up on premium strains, a quality vaporizer makes them last 3x longer than smoking. Our buyer’s guide covers all five POTV picks.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is Green Wednesday 2026?

Green Wednesday 2026 is Wednesday, November 25, 2026 — the day before Thanksgiving. It’s an annual cannabis industry sales event where dispensaries and online retailers run their biggest discounts of the year.

What’s the difference between Green Wednesday and Black Friday for cannabis?

Green Wednesday typically has the deepest dispensary discounts because customers want to stock up before the Thanksgiving holiday. Black Friday and Cyber Monday extend the deals through the weekend, with online vaporizer retailers often running their biggest sales across all four days. The deepest cuts are usually on Green Wednesday itself.

Are dispensary deals or online vape deals better?

They’re different categories. Dispensaries discount flower, edibles, concentrates, and pre-rolls. Online retailers like Planet of the Vapes discount vaporizers, accessories, and gear. The smartest shoppers cross-shop both: stock up on flower at a dispensary and buy the vaporizer to enjoy it from POTV during the same week.

Should I wait for Cyber Monday instead of Green Wednesday?

Some retailers extend their Green Wednesday deals through Cyber Monday, but the deepest cuts are typically released on Green Wednesday itself. Popular vaporizers (Storz & Bickel Venty, Mighty+) often sell out at the deepest discount level. If you have a specific product in mind, buy on Green Wednesday. If you’re flexible, Cyber Monday sometimes has surprise additional discounts on remaining inventory.

Do POTV deals require a coupon code?

It varies year to year. Some Planet of the Vapes Green Wednesday deals are automatic (price drops at checkout), while others require entering a promo code. We update this page with the exact codes when POTV announces them in October and November.

Are Green Wednesday cannabis deals available online or only in-store?

Both. Vaporizer retailers run online-only sales (POTV ships nationwide). Dispensaries vary by state: in Ohio, you must purchase in-person, but you can pre-order online for express pickup, which lets you skip the lines on Green Wednesday morning.

What time do Green Wednesday deals go live?

Most online retailers start at midnight Eastern. POTV typically opens pre-orders at 10pm Eastern the night before. Dispensaries open at their normal time but the busiest hours are 9am–1pm.
