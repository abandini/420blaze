Why this tool exists & how the matching works
Distillate edibles can’t carry a strain’s terpene profile (the terpenes are cooked off in processing). Only cold-process, full-spectrum products — live rosin, live resin, RSO — keep a flower-like profile. This Decoder finds those, matches them to a strain or feeling you like, and lets you dial the dose in mg (the only thing that “tracks” in an edible — THC% doesn’t translate). Strain Finder →
