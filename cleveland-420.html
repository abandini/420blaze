Cleveland 420 FAQs

Is cannabis legal in Cleveland, Ohio?

Medical marijuana is legal in Ohio for qualifying patients with a medical card. Recreational use remains illegal as of 2025, but possession of small amounts has been decriminalized in many areas of Cleveland.

Do I need to be 21+ to attend 420 events in Cleveland?

Yes, most 420 events in Cleveland require attendees to be 21+ with valid ID.

Can I consume cannabis at these events?

Public consumption of cannabis remains illegal in Ohio. Most events prohibit smoking indoors. Always check the specific rules of each event before attending.
