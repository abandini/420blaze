Cleveland 420 FAQs

Is cannabis legal in Cleveland, Ohio?

Yes! Ohio legalized adult-use (recreational) cannabis in November 2023 when voters approved Issue 2. Recreational sales began in August 2024. Adults 21+ can purchase up to 2.5 oz of flower and possess up to 15g of concentrates. Home cultivation is legal (up to 6 plants per person, 12 per household). Public consumption remains restricted — always check venue-specific rules.

Do I need to be 21+ to attend 420 events in Cleveland?

Yes, most 420 events in Cleveland require attendees to be 21+ with valid ID.

Can I consume cannabis at these events?

Public consumption of cannabis is still restricted under Ohio law. However, many 420 events have designated consumption areas or are held at private venues where consumption is permitted. Always check the specific rules of each event before attending.

How can I get updates about new 420 events in Cleveland?
